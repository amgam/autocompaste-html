Instructions

Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

During this experiment, you'lll be undergoing a series of trials involving copy-pasting text using a traditional(Ctrl-C, Ctrl-V) method and an alternate method called AutoComPaste.

Traditionally, one would have to navigate to a specific block of text before proceeding to copy-paste it. Instead, AutoComPaste allows you to summon relevant excerpts of texts from the current open windows contextually as you type.

To get a better idea of how this works, watch this video!

During the experiment, you'll be presented with a text block of varying length to copy from one of the windows on the right side of your screen. Your task will be to copy the relevant text using the prescribed method to the text box on the left side of the screen.

Taking a break in between experiments is advised and this section should take you about 20 minutes to complete. If you need any assistance during the course of the session, kindly notify us and we will help you as possible!

Once again, we appreciate your participation and do feel free to leave any thoughts/feedback at the end!
